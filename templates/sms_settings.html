How It Works
Daily Schedule

SMS notifications are sent automatically every night at 11:00 PM with the previous day's sales summary.

Summary Includes

Total revenue, number of transactions, boxes sold, and top-selling products from the previous day.

Privacy & Security

Your phone number is stored securely and only used for sending sales summaries. No personal data is shared.

Setup Instructions
SMS Service Setup Required

To enable SMS notifications, the following environment variables must be configured:

  • TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID - Your Twilio Account SID
  • TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN - Your Twilio Auth Token
  • TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER - Your Twilio phone number

Note: Install Twilio with: pip install twilio
