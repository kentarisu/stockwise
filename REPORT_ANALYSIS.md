# StockWise Reports Analysis: Current vs. Real Business Reports

## Executive Summary

After analyzing your current reports and cross-referencing with industry-standard business report requirements, **your reports are MISSING several critical components** that make a comprehensive, professional business report.

---

## Current Reports Structure

### 1. **Sales Summary Tab**
**Current Columns:**
- Date
- Product
- Quantity (Size)
- Boxes Sold
- Revenue
- Profit

**What's Missing:**
- ❌ **Gross Margin %** (Profit/Revenue ratio)
- ❌ **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)** breakdown
- ❌ **Unit Cost** vs **Unit Price** comparison
- ❌ **Sales Growth %** (period-over-period comparison)
- ❌ **VAT breakdown** (you calculate it but don't show it separately)
- ❌ **Transaction count** per product
- ❌ **Average transaction value** per product

### 2. **Top Products Tab**
**Current Columns:**
- Rank
- Product
- Quantity (Size)
- Boxes Sold
- Avg. Price
- Revenue

**What's Missing:**
- ❌ **Profit Margin %** (critical for profitability analysis)
- ❌ **Growth Rate** (vs previous period)
- ❌ **Market Share %** (product revenue / total revenue)
- ❌ **Units Sold Trend** (increasing/decreasing)
- ❌ **Inventory Turnover** (how fast it sells)

### 3. **Low Stock Tab**
**Current Columns:**
- Date
- Product
- Quantity (Size)
- Current Stock
- Price
- Status
- Action Required

**What's Missing:**
- ❌ **Days of Supply** (how many days until out of stock at current sales rate)
- ❌ **Reorder Point** (when to reorder)
- ❌ **Reorder Quantity** (how much to order)
- ❌ **Last Sale Date** (when was it last sold)
- ❌ **Average Daily Sales** (sales velocity)
- ❌ **Lead Time** (time to receive new stock)
- ❌ **Stock Value** (current stock × cost)

### 4. **Transactions Tab**
**Current Columns:**
- Sale No.
- Date & Time
- Customer
- Products
- Quantity (Size)
- Boxes
- Total
- Recorded By
- Status
- View Details

**What's Missing:**
- ❌ **Tax/VAT Amount** (separate line)
- ❌ **Subtotal** (before tax)
- ❌ **Receipt Number** (OR Number is there but not clearly labeled)

## What a REAL Business Report Should Include

### **Essential Components Missing:**

#### 1. **Financial Metrics**
- ✅ Revenue (you have this)
- ✅ Profit (you have this)
- ❌ **Gross Margin %** - CRITICAL for profitability analysis
- ❌ **Net Profit** (after all expenses)
- ❌ **COGS Breakdown** - Show cost vs revenue clearly
- ❌ **Profit Margin by Product** - Which products are most profitable?

#### 2. **Sales Performance Metrics**
- ✅ Total Revenue (you have this)
- ✅ Average Sale (you have this)
- ❌ **Sales Growth %** - Period-over-period comparison
- ❌ **Sales Velocity** - Units sold per day/week
- ❌ **Conversion Rate** - If you track leads/quotes

#### 3. **Inventory Analysis**
- ✅ Current Stock (you have this)
- ✅ Low Stock Alerts (you have this)
- ❌ **Inventory Turnover Ratio** - How fast inventory sells
- ❌ **Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)** - How long inventory sits
- ❌ **Stock Aging Report** - Old inventory that's not moving
- ❌ **Dead Stock** - Products with no sales in X days
- ❌ **ABC Analysis** - Classify products by value (A=high, B=medium, C=low)

#### 5. **Product Performance**
- ✅ Top Products (you have this)
- ❌ **Product Profitability Ranking** - Rank by profit, not just revenue
- ❌ **Slow Movers** - Products with low sales
- ❌ **Product Mix Analysis** - Which products sell together
- ❌ **Price Elasticity** - How price changes affect sales
- ❌ **Seasonal Trends** - Sales patterns by month/season

#### 6. **Operational Metrics**
- ✅ Transactions Count (you have this)
- ❌ **Void/Return Rate** - How many transactions are voided
- ❌ **Peak Sales Days** - When do you sell most?
