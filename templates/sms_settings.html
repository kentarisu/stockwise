SMS Service Setup
SMS Service Configuration Required

To enable SMS notifications, configure these environment variables:

  • TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID - Your Twilio Account SID
  • TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN - Your Twilio Auth Token
  • TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER - Your Twilio phone number

Installation: pip install twilio

Phone Number Verification Required

If you're using a Twilio trial account, you must verify your phone number:

  1. Go to Twilio Console
  2. Navigate to "Phone Numbers" ‚Üí "Manage" ‚Üí "Verified Caller IDs"
  3. Add your phone number: +639630675254
  4. Verify with the SMS code sent to your phone

Alternative: Upgrade to a paid Twilio account to send to any number.
